ABOUT

Hi, I'm Courtney.

I like design, what can I say. I specifically like working with people who use design for good and not just the bottom line. If your aim is to support your community, I'm all in.

A person of few words, but precise communication skills. My hunger for learning gives me the drive to consistently improve and a desire to take on new projects that will allow me to grow and contribute. I maintain a hardworking attitude while taking a hands-on approach to finding clean and creative solutions. Designing for good is my passion and it brings me joy to know that I can contribute to bettering whichever community I'm supporting.

When I'm not designing or illustrating, you can find me out on the trails either mountain biking or hiking, reading a good book, or cooking up a storm.
